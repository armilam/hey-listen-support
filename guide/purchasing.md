# Purchasing

Hey Listen is available as a one-time purchase with a free trial.

---

## Free Trial

Hey Listen includes a **14-day free trial** with full access to all features. No payment information is required to start the trial. When you first launch the app, the trial begins automatically.

---

## Purchasing

After the trial ends, a purchase prompt appears the next time an alert would fire. You can purchase from the prompt or at any time from **Settings → About**.

Hey Listen is a **one-time purchase** — there is no subscription. Pay once and use it indefinitely, including future updates.

---

## Existing Users

If you installed Hey Listen before the free trial was introduced, you are automatically granted permanent access at no cost. You will not see trial expiration prompts.

---

## Restoring a Purchase

If you reinstall Hey Listen or switch to a new Mac, your purchase is tied to your Apple ID. Open **Settings → About** and use the **Restore Purchase** option to unlock the app without paying again.

---
